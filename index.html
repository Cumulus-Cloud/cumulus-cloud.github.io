Personal cloud done right!


Our vision of the self-hosted cloud, done right. Using state of the art technologies and frameworks, Cumulus aims to be the flawless experience you always wanted. Easy to deploy, fast, secure and with a beautiful UI.

And the best? It's open-source!

Made with the best

Functionnal and reactive from the front-end to the back-end, Cumulus has been crafted with modern technologies, making the most of what they have to offer. Scalable and robust, Play Framework and Akka make possible for a lightweight, stateless and secure server. React with Redux allowed us to build an awesome single page application as the main way to interact with your own personnal cloud.

It's beautiful

An app without an appealing UI is nothing. Our webapp's design has been our main concern from day one. With the help of professional & passionate designers, we crafted an unique experience, taking the best of every other existing solutions.

User experience is also one of our main concern, as we tried to deliver a seamless experience while still providing all the functionnality you expect from a personal cloud.

Easy to use

Self hosting sometime means headache to install. We aimed to be able to install, deploy, configure and start using Cumulus with little to no technical knowledge.

.. and of course, if you still want to get your hands dirty you'll still be able to fine-tune everything. That's the beauty of open-source projects.

Powerfull

Cumulus also try to provide everything you ever wanted from self hosted cloud. Account adminsitration, multiple storage sources, security and ease to use.

Features

Secure

Using a AES-256 ciphering, your data is safe and sound in whatever storage option you choose.

Your personal key is never stored, and derived from your password. This means that even with your database, your files, or both, someone we'll never be able to obtain anything!

Fast

Altough depending on the app server and on the storage servers, we always kept in mind the rapidity and efficiency of our application.

The reactive non-blocking approach of Akka ensure that no computation power will be wasted, and that we make the most of what is available.

Free & Open-Source

All the code is open-source and licenced under the permissive MIT licence, to allow anyone to make the most of Cumulus, and even to change it to its own tastes.

You can also contribute to the project using the provided tools by Github.
